Hit Pump was established in May 2018 by Konstantin Sigachev and Ilya Black in Auckland. They started performing as an acoustic duet and slowly evolved into a full scale rock band. In October 2019 Hit Pump with their current members performed live and gathered over 450 guests and many more could not get in because the show was Sold Out! They have built a reputation for head-spinning live shows and continue to develop and experiment with different genres.
